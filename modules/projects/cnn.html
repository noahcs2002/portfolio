MNIST Convolutional Neural Network

Following the tutorial of a youtuber, I recreated a Convolutional Neural Network to read MNIST Data. The goal of this project was to dip my toes into the world of AI, agents, and machine learning. I created several different layers to the network, learned about propogation, and how neural networks operate.
